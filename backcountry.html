What is Backcountry Skiing?

Backcountry skiing involves hiking up to ski down terrain outside of the resort.

The Gear

  • Skis
  • Uphill bindings/compatible boots
  • Skins
  • Beacon*
  • Shovel*
  • Probe*

* only necessary for avalanche terrain

Why Backcountry Ski?

  • Find new terrain!
  • It's a great workout!
  • Enjoy the outdoors!
